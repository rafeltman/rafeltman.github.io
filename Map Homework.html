Where's your cheese coming from? Almost definitely Wisconsin or California. But it might surprise you to find that New York and New Mexico are also big players. And despite their cheddery fame, Vermont only produced a paltry 10,412,000 pounds of cheese in September of 2011. Guess those artisanal curds are something special.
